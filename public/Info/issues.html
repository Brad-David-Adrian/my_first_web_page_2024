The main issue of League is its balancing team.

The balancing team of League of Legends is a group of people

who are in charge of making sure that the game is fair and balanced.

They do this by making changes to the game

such as nerfing or buffing champions, items, and other aspects of the game.

However, the balancing team has been criticized for not doing a good job of balancing the game.

Many players feel that certain champions are too strong or too weak

and that the game is not fun to play as a result.

Some players have even quit the game because of the balancing issues.

Riot Games, the company that makes League of Legends,

has tried to address these concerns by making changes to the game,

but many players feel that the balancing team is not doing enough to fix the problems.
